Welcome to the Flash Card Memory Game

The rules are simple, you test your own memory with the flash cards. A card will appear and you have a selection of options to choose from. If your selection is correct, you will move on to the next card! If your selection is incorrect, you will not receive a point, but you will have the option to come back and try again later!

Take a peek at the answer by clicking on the question mark in the lower right hand corner of the card
